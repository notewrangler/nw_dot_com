About Me

A long time ago, in a galaxy known to many as "The Apple," my journey began. It's been a swerving path through many locales, years of drum & bugle corps tours (& attendant bus breakdowns), writing music for young people to play in competitions, playing the drums & bass in a variety of different settings--for dancing and for just listening--and lately? Non-stop coding & learning web technologies, to build useful 21st-Century tools for people.

Through it all, my passion has been getting things right--whether it be crafting a marching band arrangement that makes you forget you're even listening to a marching band, or kicking a big band and keeping it in a solid groove--or constructing clean, logical code that does what it's supposed to do, in as efficient a manner as possible. Going the extra mile, keeping an eye on the big picture, and making sure the details are covered as well as can be--that's my paradigm.
